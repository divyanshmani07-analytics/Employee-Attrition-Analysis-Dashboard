# 📊 Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

### 👥 HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

An interactive and insightful Power BI dashboard developed to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, job satisfaction levels, and retention patterns. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable business insights, helping organizations make informed workforce and retention decisions.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution designed to help organizations understand why employees leave and identify factors influencing workforce turnover. Through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and detailed workforce analysis, the dashboard supports HR professionals in improving employee retention, workforce planning, and organizational performance.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Used for dashboard creation and interactive reporting.<br>
• 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.<br>
• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for creating KPIs, calculated measures, and business logic.<br>
• 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between employee-related dimensions for efficient reporting.<br>
• 📈 **Data Visualization** – Interactive charts and visuals for workforce insights.<br>
• 📁 **File Format** – `.pbit` for dashboard template and `.png` for dashboard previews.

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Employee attrition is a significant challenge for organizations because it directly affects productivity, operational efficiency, recruitment costs, and workforce stability.

Key business questions include:

* What is the overall employee attrition rate?
* Which departments experience the highest turnover?
* Which job roles are most affected by attrition?
* How does salary influence employee retention?
* What is the relationship between job satisfaction and employee turnover?
* Which age groups have the highest attrition rates?
* How does employee tenure impact attrition?

Without an effective analytical solution, identifying these patterns becomes difficult and time-consuming.

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

The primary objectives of the dashboard are to:

* Monitor workforce attrition through key HR metrics.
* Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
* Analyze workforce demographics and retention trends.
* Support strategic HR decision-making.
* Improve employee retention initiatives.
* Provide a centralized and interactive reporting solution.
